What is Vegetarianism?

In short, vegetarianism is the practice of not consuming meat, poultry, or fish. It is a diet that primarily consists of plant-based foods.

Benefits of Vegetarianism

There are many benefits to adopting a vegetarian diet, including improved health, reduced environmental impact, and ethical considerations.
