Definition of Vegetarianism

Vegetarianism is a dietary and lifestyle choice that involves abstaining from the consumption of meat, poultry, and fish. It is a plant-based diet that may include dairy and eggs for some individuals.

Types of Vegetarian Diets

click on the names of each type of diet to learn more about them.

There are various types of vegetarian diets, including:

  • Lacto-Ovo Vegetarian: Includes dairy products and eggs but avoids meat and fish.
  • Lacto-Vegetarian: Includes dairy products but avoids meat, fish, and eggs.
  • Ovo-Vegetarian: Includes eggs but avoids meat, fish, and dairy products.
  • Vegan: Excludes all animal products, including meat, dairy, eggs, and sometimes honey (although this type of plant-based diet does not constitute as vegetarianism, it is often associated with vegetarianism).
